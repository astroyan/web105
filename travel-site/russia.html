I have lived in Russia for a total of about seven months. For one month I lived in St. Petersburg, and the rest I lived in Moscow. Some of the cities that I traveled to in the Russian Federation include Tula, Volgograd, Kazan, Peterhof, Tsarskoye Selo, and Sergiyev Posad.

My favorite part of traveling around Russia was the train rides. Russia has an incredible public transport and train system. The cross-country trains have cabins with four beds each, and often when our group would travel the two bottom bunks would belong to strangers. Those were the most interesting conversations, as the average Russian knows little to no English and most likely has never met anyone from America.

Russia is an incredibly beautiful country with an immense history and warm people. I would love to return there someday and travel to even more cities and talk with even more people on the trains.
