I decided that I wanted to make a website dedicated to some of my favorite woodland animals.

The house that I grew up in was (and is) surrounded by forests, and the Appalachian Mountains in general are covered in trees. Consistent encounters with the critters that live in the woods made me appreciate them greatly, and seeing my favorite animals always sparks joy.

The animals that I picked - squirrels, possums, and rabbits - are ones that are also common in the Midwest.
